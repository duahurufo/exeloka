How does the AI cultural analysis work?

Our system uses TensorFlow.js for quick analysis and OpenRouter API for advanced cultural understanding. It analyzes your project context and provides culturally-sensitive recommendations.

What types of documents can I upload?

You can upload PDFs, images (for OCR), and provide URLs. The system automatically extracts content and analyzes it for cultural insights.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we follow best security practices with encrypted connections, secure authentication, and privacy-first data handling.

Recommended Document Types:

  • Cultural Guidelines: Community protocols, traditional practices, social norms
  • Research Papers: Academic studies on local culture, anthropological research
