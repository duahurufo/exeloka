Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account with Exeloka, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Organization details
  • Project information and cultural context
  • Knowledge sources and documents you upload

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of our service:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, access times)
  • Feature usage and interaction patterns
  • AI recommendation feedback and ratings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our cultural intelligence services
  • Generate personalized cultural recommendations
  • Analyze and improve AI model accuracy
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Respond to support requests and feedback

3. Cultural Sensitivity & Data Protection

We understand that cultural information requires special care:

  • Cultural context data is processed with enhanced security measures
  • Sensitive cultural information is not shared with third parties
  • We work with Indonesian cultural experts to ensure appropriate handling
  • All cultural analysis is performed with respect for local traditions

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who help operate our service (with strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger or acquisition (with continued privacy protection)

5. AI and Machine Learning

Our AI systems process your data to provide cultural recommendations:

  • TensorFlow.js processes data locally in your browser when possible
  • OpenRouter API is used for advanced analysis with encrypted transmission
  • AI training may use anonymized, aggregated data patterns
  • You can opt-out of AI improvement programs in your settings

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Employee training on data privacy and cultural sensitivity

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your project and knowledge data
  • Objection: Opt-out of certain data processing activities

8. International Data Transfers

Our services may involve data processing in multiple countries:

  • Primary data storage is located in secure Indonesian data centers
  • AI processing may occur in secured international facilities
  • All international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or through our service.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices:

  • Email: privacy@exeloka.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Support
  • Mail: Exeloka Privacy Team, Jl. Raya Sampang No. 123, Sampang, East Java 69216, Indonesia

This privacy policy is designed to be transparent about our data practices while respecting Indonesian cultural values and international privacy standards.
